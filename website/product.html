Large Silage Fork

Features:

  • Heavy duty tines 32" long at 9" spacing 4' - 8' wide
  • With or without top grab
  • Fully enclosed or with 'see through' back
  • Any colour you wish

Suitable for:

  • Agriculture
  • Waste Handling
  • Tractor
  • Telehandler
  • Backhoe

The Murray Grain Pusher is designed to push grain considerably higher than is possible with a standard grain bucket. It consists of a blade which is attached to a long box section frame with Tractor 'Loader or Telescopic Handler mounting brackets at the opposite end.

The Murray Large Silage Fork is designed for extra capacity when loading chopped grass onto a silage pit. The frame is more heavily constructed and is fitted with extra duty tines bolted into large tapered sockets fitted along the bottom and the sides of the silage fork.
