Our History

The history of Murray Machinery

Over 30 years of engineering excellence

Established near Aberdeen (UK) in 1979, Murray Machinery are UK agricultural machinery manufacturers who specialise in material handling attachments to fit telescopic handlers, forklifts and loaders.

At Murray Machinery we are able to offer the benefit of over 30 years experience in the design and development of all our different attachments. Feedback from our customers is extremely valuable in the evolution of our products to keep up with the ever changing methods and techniques used in the agricultural industry.
